    President College

    President College is situated in the village of Fort, 200 meters from the historic Dutch Fort in the Matara District in the Southern Province.

    It was commenced in 1817 in the a area of 30 perches near the Nilwala River, Wilfred Gunasekara Mawatha from the south , the Methodist Church from the East and a private house from the West.

    This school, which was started as Mr / Methodist Jennings School, was controlled by the Methodist Council priests and only Christian children were admitted. Sinhala Buddhist children did not have room to enter the school, and the school was started with three teachers and 80 students.

    The main building that was started as a missionary school is also at present can be seen in the same shape.

    On the 17th November 1960, it was taken over by the government with the name of Mr/Fort Primary school. As facilities, only a water pipe system was maintained in the school at the beginning. This school, consisting of a small teacher's quarters and one building, was introduced to accommodate Sinhala Buddhist children. The pioneer principal sir was Mr. Lionel De silwa and he started conducting the school classes even in the teachers' quarters as the land was limited in 1960 .

    from 1960, there were many principals serving here and among them Mrs.B. Jinadasa and Mr. D. Sudusinghe are distinguished principals.

    We have many victories. Out of them the second place in 2009 National Productivity awards from the entire island and the first place in 2012 environmental-friendly school competition. This school has the highest student population in the least land area .

    Principal Madam B. Jinadasa Era 1972 - 1995

       When Mrs. B.Jinadasa assumed duties, there were 38 students and five teachers. Later two teachers were transferred on the grounds that there was no necessity of English teachers to the school. Daily attendance of the students was 18 or 19 out of 38. For this reason, in 1972 grade six class was removed. Grade 7 class and grade 8 class were removed in 1973 and 1974. At that time , the school admittance age was increased up to 6 years and because of that grade 1 class was removed in 1972.

         So, Mrs. B.Jinadasa led this school to a new path and in 1973, she started the grade 1 class. At the same time the classes were increased up to grade 8. In 1994 grade 9 class was started and she retired in that year.

         By 1995 , there were 325 students and 25 teachers.During her era, a 26 perch land and the Fisheries Office were acquired to the school land. The present principle's office is situated there. It was a tiresome effort to the principal madam and the staff to meet the political leaders and ministers for this purpose.

         Further , during this era Literary Societies, Educational Trips and other Extra Curricular Activities were started under the limited resources. Those who passed grade 5 scholarship exam had the chance even entering Ananda College in Colombo.

    Principal D.Sudusinghe Era 1995 - 2014

      Mr . D .Sudusinghe as a principal was friendly Kind and for sighted. It is obvious that the golden area of Janadhipathi vidyalaya was during his tenure. As a science teacher he assumed duties in this school on 15 August 1993. He was a graduate and had a post graduation and education.

         As his initial step the school was named as Janadhipathi Vidyalaya. he started his principal service career as a grade Z principal on 10 th June 1995. In 1995 students faced the G.C.E O/L exam from this school. He started A/L Arts stream in 1999 thus making it a 1C school. A/L Commerce stream was started in 2001. Another land mark was that the school received the recommendation of the ministry of education as an entrance school grade 5 scholarship holders. The first three strayed building was built in 1996 anther three storeyed and four streyed buildings in 2001 and in 2010 new main hall with the auditorium was built.

         In numerous occasions Mr.D. Sudusinghe was awarded .In 1999 he received a Merit award from Godawithana foundation in 2006 "Vidya Niketha Keerthi" in 2008 the best principal of the southern province and in 2014 he was among the 25 principals who were selected as the best in all island level.

         Janadhipathi Vidyalaya become a 1AB School in 2011 with the start of the A/L Science stream under the guidance of this principal sir.He was responsible for the upliftment of grade 5 scholarship results G.C.E O/L results and G.C.E A/L results in 2009 he implemented 6S programme in the school and it received the seven place in all island level. In the same year 5S programme was launched and it received the first place in the southern province and the second place in all island level.

         He always took the sensory measures to develop the school with the combination of modern technology and sinhala culture. In this era computer unit and the media unit the inaugurated. So it is evident that Mr.D. Sudusinghe was the principal who dedicated all the effort and commitment for the betterment of Janadhipathi Vidyalaya
